Our Story

About Veloura

A journey rooted in passion, driven by quality, and committed to the art of exceptional coffee.

Since 2012

From a Single Bean
to a Global Vision

Veloura Coffee was born in a small Brooklyn roastery, where a group of friends shared a simple belief: coffee should be extraordinary. What began as a weekend passion quickly grew into a mission — to source the world's finest beans and roast them with uncompromising care.

Over a decade later, Veloura has grown from a local favorite into a celebrated name in specialty coffee, serving communities across the country while staying true to our roots of craft, quality, and authenticity.

Our Promise

Quality Without Compromise

At Veloura, quality isn't a goal — it's a standard. From the altitude of the farms we source from, to the precise temperature of every roast, every variable is controlled to ensure your cup is perfect.

We roast in small batches to preserve the integrity of each bean's unique profile. Our team tastes every batch before it's approved, ensuring consistency across every bag we sell.
